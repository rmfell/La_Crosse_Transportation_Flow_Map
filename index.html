La Crosse Transportation Flow Map

About

This map is the final product of an undergradute research project. The research project was a collaboration between the University of Wisconsin-La Crosse's Computer Science and Computer Engineering Department and the Geography and Environmental Science Department.This project used survey data collected from local employers, the survey recorded data about people's commute to and from work. We analyzed this data to create flow maps showing the most utilized routes in La Crosse.
